Start Your Workday Comfortably

Get in, sit back and experience the comfort of car that takes you directly to your destination. Arriving work as a sardine with public transport or sweating is a thing of the past

How to get started?

  • Create you account

    Complete your account with some basic information including you home your work location and your working hours.

  • Plan your journeys

    Open the app, click the agenda tab and enter your working hours for the coming week. Then you'll be presented with a list of ours drivers.

  • Meet your driver

    Use our chat to get to know your match with our driver. You'll be picked up at your regular location, or agree on an alternative pick up location.

  • Enjoy your journey

    Get in, sit back and enjoy your journey that takes you directly to your destination with some free cup of coffe from us.

DriveOn is a mobile carpool platform that effortlessly brings commuter together in one car.
Traveling home and work will never be the same again. Our mission is to stop traffic jams and C02 pollution as a result of inefficient car commuting.
